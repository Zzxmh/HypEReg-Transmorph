Dear Editor of \textit{Journal of Imaging},

\vspace{0.75em}

We submit the manuscript entitled ``HypEReg-TransMorph: A Hyperelastic-Regularized Transformer Framework for Folding-Suppressed Deformable Brain MRI Registration'' for consideration as a research article.

The work addresses deformable brain MRI registration with a Transformer-based dense registration backbone and training-time hyperelastic-energy regularization (HypEReg) to improve Jacobian regularity and folding suppression while maintaining competitive overlap metrics on a public IXI atlas-to-subject protocol. We believe the topic aligns with the journal's scope in medical imaging and artificial intelligence for imaging, including methodological contributions relevant to neuroimaging pipelines.
